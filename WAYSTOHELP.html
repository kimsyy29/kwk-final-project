Ways to Help

You can help in many ways, such as raising awareness with your friends and peers, donating to charities, or volunteering at non-profit organizations to help those in need of resources!

UNICEF!

One great charity that helps people all over the world is UNICEF. UNICEF's mission is to support vulnerable children and provide them with nutrition, water, and education opportunities they otherwise might not have a chance to access.
